--- layout: page title: Hi, I'm Kak Soky subtitle: PhD student in Automatic Speech Recognition use-site-title: true --- Short Bio

I am a second-year PhD student at Kyoto University, working in the Speech and Audio Processing Lab. (kawaharalab), supervised by Prof. Tatsuya Kawahara. My research interests lie in the application of machine learning methods for speech and language tasks.
I am currently working on:
  • Multilingual and Khmer automatic speech recognition (ASR)
  • Spoken language translation of Khmer, English and French, using a court dataset namely: ECCC.
