--- layout: page title: Hi, I'm Kak Soky subtitle: PhD student in Automatic Speech Recognition use-site-title: true --- Short Bio

I am a PhD student at Kyoto University, working in the Speech and Audio Processing Lab. (kawaharalab), supervised by Prof. Tatsuya Kawahara. My research interests lie in the application of machine learning methods for speech and language tasks.
I am currently working on a spoken language translation corpus of Khmer, English and French, using a court dataset namely: ECCC. With this dataset, I am focusing on improving Khmer automatic speech recognition (ASR):
  • Joint training ASR and speaker recognition
  • Joint training ASR and machine/speech translation.
